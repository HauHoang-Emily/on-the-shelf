Happily Never After

$16.91
by Lynn Painter


Sophie Steinbeck, upon discovering her fiancé's infidelity, hesitates to call off the wedding due to her father's powerful boss being the father-in-law. Enter Max, a professional objector who disrupts weddings by objecting. Sophie becomes intrigued by Max's mission to prevent people from making life-altering mistakes. They team up, crashing weddings and finding a shared thrill in their antics. Despite their initial skepticism about love, their undeniable chemistry leads to a series of hookups. However, their dynamic shifts when they encounter a wedding where the bride is Max's former flame. As they navigate this situation, Sophie realizes her growing feelings for Max.

About the Author

Lynn Painter is the international bestselling author of Better Than The Movies. She writes romantic comedies for both teens and adults, and when she isn't reading or writing, odds are good she’s guzzling energy drinks and watching rom-coms.
