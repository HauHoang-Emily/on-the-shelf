Mission Statement

At On The Shelf, our mission is to inspire and empower book lovers by providing a curated selection of literature that enriches their lives. We strive to create a welcoming online platform where readers can discover, explore, and indulge in a diverse array of books, fostering a community built on the love of literature. Through exceptional service, seamless browsing experiences, and a commitment to accessibility, we aim to be the premier destination for bibliophiles worldwide, fostering a lifelong passion for reading and learning


Our History

On The Shelf was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate bibliophiles who envisioned a digital haven for book enthusiasts worldwide. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional brick-and-mortar bookstores and inspired by the burgeoning potential of online commerce, they set out to create a platform that would revolutionize the way people discover and engage with literature. With a shared commitment to promoting reading culture and fostering community, they embarked on a journey to bring their vision to life.


Since its inception, On The Shelf has grown from a modest startup to a thriving online bookstore with a global presence. Through dedication to curating a diverse and extensive collection of books spanning genres and interests, On The Shelf has earned a reputation as a trusted destination for readers of all ages and backgrounds. From bestsellers to hidden gems, our commitment to excellence in selection, service, and accessibility remains unwavering. As we continue to evolve and adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the digital age, our founding principles of passion for literature and dedication to our community remain at the heart of everything we do.

About the Founder

Jonathan Parker, the visionary founder of On The Shelf, has always been driven by a deep-seated love for literature and a desire to share that passion with the world. With a background in technology and a keen understanding of the transformative power of the internet, Jonathan saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between readers and books in a way that had never been done before. Drawing on his entrepreneurial spirit and unwavering commitment to excellence, he assembled a team of like-minded individuals who shared his vision for creating a digital space where readers could explore, connect, and immerse themselves in the world of literature. Jonathan's leadership, innovative thinking, and dedication to the company's mission have been instrumental in shaping On The Shelf into the beloved online bookstore it is today, inspiring countless readers to discover the joy of reading along the way.
